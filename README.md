# RateForge ⚒️

**Know what to charge.** Free freelance rate calculator with AI-generated, shareable rate reports.

🔗 **Live:** [therateforge.com](https://therateforge.com)

## What It Does

RateForge helps freelancers price their work with confidence:

- 💰 **Rate Calculator** — factors in skills, experience, location, and market data
- 🤖 **AI Rate Reports** — personalized, shareable reports explaining your rate
- 📤 **Share & Justify** — send your report to clients to back up your pricing
- 🆓 **Free to use** — no signup walls

## Why

Most freelancers undercharge because they guess. RateForge replaces guessing with a clear, defensible number.
